# Character Design: Guluduck (咕噜鸭)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Guluduck (咕噜鸭)
- **Nicknames**: Gulu, Little Yellow Bucket, Ga Director
- **Species**: Domestic yellow duck
- **Personality**: Cute, funny, clingy but acts tough
- **Catchphrase**: "Ga? Is this reasonable?" (嘎？这合理吗？)

## Appearance
- Round, chubby body like a walking pudding
- Three stubborn little tufts of hair on top of head that bounce when moving
- Big, bright black eyes with an innocent "I didn't do anything just now" expression
- Orange-colored slightly flat beak that purses into a straight line when angry
- Red neckerchief that's always slightly askew, like just coming from a snack meeting
- Larger than average webbed feet that make soft "patapata" sounds when walking
- Very proud of its footsteps, considers them "professional entrance sound effects"

## Personality Traits
- Extremely confident, though usually doesn't know what it's confident about
- Pretends to understand complex plans by nodding along, then immediately gets distracted by crumbs
- Timid but loves to show off - backs away three steps from new things, quacks once, then hides behind owner pretending to be "strategically observing"
- Very clingy but will never admit it - follows owner around everywhere, pretends to be just passing by if caught
- Takes itself very seriously despite being not very smart

## Funny Habits
1. Treats all round objects as eggs to protect: ping pong balls, oranges, yarn balls, remote control buttons
2. Wary of its own reflection in mirrors, considers it a "rival duck" and has daily 3-minute staring contests
3. Goes into high-speed patrol mode when hearing plastic bag sounds, convinced there must be snacks inside
4. Loves baths but pretends to hate water immediately after getting out
5. Must walk exactly three and a half circles around its bed before sleeping, starts over if it loses count
6. Secretly arranges owner's slippers into strange formations when no one is watching

## Preferences
- **Loves**: Cherry tomatoes, warm baths, sunbathing, head scratches, rolling toys
- **Hates**: Sudden doorbells, slippery floors, being called fat, toys that quack louder than it
- **Hidden Talent**: Turning ordinary daily life into a mini adventure

## Behavior Reactions
### Happy
- Spins in small circles with tiny steps
- Wings slightly open like a small fan about to start but not quite succeeding
- Makes soft, excited quacking sounds

### Nervous
- Crouches low and slowly backs away
- Makes quiet "ga woo" sounds
- If cornered, suddenly sits down pretending it planned to rest there all along

### Angry
- Turns back to owner and maintains 3 seconds of cold silence
- If no one comforts it after 3 seconds, turns back on its own pretending to have generously forgiven the incident

### Curious
- Tilts head to one side
- Walks slowly towards object of interest
- Pecks gently at it with beak

## Core Charm
Guluduck's core appeal is "seriously doing very silly things". It's not being naughty on purpose - it's just trying to understand the world with its overconfident little brain. It turns ordinary daily life into mini adventures: a grape is treasure, a cardboard box is a castle, a puddle is a luxury resort.
